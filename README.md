## What is Motion?  

Motion is Apple’s motion design application that allows creators to produce breathtaking graphics, titles, and effects. Built to integrate perfectly with Final Cut Pro, it’s a must-have tool for those working in video post-production and animation.  

From elegant text animations to realistic particle systems, Motion gives you the creative control you need. It supports full 3D environments, multiple camera setups, and keyframe-based animation — all while running smoothly thanks to Metal acceleration.  

Whether you're enhancing your next film project, creating social media visuals, or building broadcast graphics, Motion offers both power and simplicity to elevate your work.  

### Key Highlights  

* Real-time motion design and effects creation  
* Seamless Final Cut Pro integration  
* Optimized for Apple Silicon (M1, M2, M3)  
* Support for 3D text, depth, and lighting
